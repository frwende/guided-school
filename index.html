The Guided Method
How it works
Seven things that make Guided different from every other AI tool your child has tried.
1
Set up in 2 minutes
Parent, teacher, or student — pick your path. A parent creates a profile with their child's name, age, subjects, and school. A teacher sets up an entire class with PINs and learning goals. A student onboards themselves in 4 steps. Guided automatically adapts its language, analogies, and curriculum to match.
2
Ask anything
Homework problem, confusing concept, exam prep. Type it, paste it, or snap a photo of the page. Guided understands German, Swiss, UK, US, French, Italian, and IB curricula — so it knows what your school actually teaches.
3
Guided never gives the answer
This is what makes us fundamentally different. Instead of solving it for your child, Guided asks the right questions — leading them step by step to figure it out themselves. Not because we're withholding information. Because real understanding only happens when you think it through yourself.
4
When they're stuck, the Hint System helps
Three levels of hints per session, each a little more specific. Your child decides how much help they need — and learns to push through difficulty before reaching for the next one.
5
Every breakthrough gets recognised
When your child arrives at the answer on their own, Guided celebrates the moment — names exactly what they just figured out, explains why it matters, and immediately raises the bar with a harder question. We call these AHA moments. You'll see every single one.
6
You see the full picture
The Parent Dashboard tracks Bloom's Taxonomy levels in real time — from basic recall to deep analysis and creative thinking. You see session duration, hint usage, AHA moments, and whether your child is actually thinking or just going through the motions. No hovering required.
7
For schools: the tutor that knows today's lesson
Teachers upload what they covered in class — a quick note, a worksheet, a photo of the board. Guided uses this so that when a student asks for help that evening, the tutor knows exactly what was taught, what was assigned, and what comes next. No other AI tool can do this.
Free to try — no credit card required.
Legal & Safety
Important Disclaimer
Please read this before relying on Guided for important decisions.
AI Can Make Mistakes
Guided uses AI and may produce incorrect, incomplete, or outdated answers. Always verify important information with a qualified teacher, tutor, or professional source. Do not rely solely on Guided for exam preparation or critical academic decisions.
Not a Substitute for Professional Advice
Guided is an educational support tool — not a replacement for qualified tutors, teachers, therapists, or medical professionals. If a student is struggling seriously with learning, mental health, or wellbeing, please seek qualified professional support.
No Liability
Guided and its operators accept no responsibility for decisions made based on content generated by the app. Use of Guided is entirely at your own risk. We make no warranties about the accuracy, completeness, or fitness of AI-generated responses for any particular purpose.
Parental Responsibility
Guided is designed for students aged 8–18. Parents and guardians are responsible for supervising their child's use of the app and ensuring it is used appropriately. Guided does not monitor individual conversations.
School Affiliation
Guided is not officially affiliated with any school, government education authority, or examination board unless a formal partnership agreement is in place. Curriculum guidance is provided in good faith based on publicly available information.
Your Data
Conversations with Guided are processed by Claude AI (Anthropic). By using Guided you agree to Anthropic's usage policies. We do not sell your data. API keys are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
